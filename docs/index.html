Who We Are

The Tikondane Foundation is a US-based non-profit arm of the Tikondane Community Centre, dedicated to supporting and empowering underprivileged communities in Katete, Eastern Province, Zambia. Our mission is to provide sustainable solutions in education, health, and community development.
